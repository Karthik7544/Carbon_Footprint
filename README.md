# Carbon Footprint

Carbon Footprint is an interactive awareness platform designed to help people understand how their everyday choices contribute to their carbon footprint and how small changes can make a meaningful environmental impact.

## About the Project

The platform guides users through a personalized journey that combines awareness, interaction, and reflection.

Users begin with an initial carbon footprint assessment and provide information about their lifestyle, habits, and location. Instead of relying only on traditional forms, the platform uses a simple gamified experience where users explore different zones and answer questions through interactive gameplay.

Based on the information provided, the platform estimates the user's annual carbon footprint and generates a personalized analysis. Users also receive a summarized story presented in three short paragraphs, helping them better understand the environmental impact of their daily decisions in a more engaging and relatable way.

At the end of the experience, users can download a compact pledge card and commit to making more sustainable choices in their daily lives.

## Key Features

* Personalized carbon footprint assessment
* City-aware analysis based on user inputs
* Interactive 2D zone-based experience for collecting lifestyle preferences
* Carbon footprint estimation and annual impact summary
* Personalized story generation
* Downloadable sustainability pledge card
* Awareness-focused and beginner-friendly experience

## Core Message

Everyday decisions matter.

The way we travel, consume, shop, and use resources can collectively create a significant impact on the environment around us. By becoming more aware of our habits, we can take small but meaningful steps toward a more sustainable future.
